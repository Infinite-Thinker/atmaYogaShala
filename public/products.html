OUR SERVICES

We offer a variety of courses to enrich body, mind and soul.
Whether you are a beginner or advanced level yoga practitioner,
or seeking mental transformation and spiritual evolution
– there is something for everyone!

Grouped Classes

STUDIO GROUP CLASSES

  • An online classroom allows students and teachers to collaborate, communicate and interact with each other over the internet.
  • These classes are a mix of recordings of video or live classes of yoga asanas that can be done at your convenience.

KIDS CLASSES

  • Yoga and mindfulness have been shown to improve both physical and mental health in school-age children (ages 6 to 12).
  • Yoga improves balance, strength, endurance, and aerobic capacity in children.

Personalised Online Classes

FIT YOGI

  • To build resilience through breath and mindfulness, you can go that extra mile with a little bit of commitment to yoga.
  • Simply pick 20 to 30 minutes a day based on your schedule.
  • The rest is assured, as this course will be designed just for you considering your particular sport and type of your body.

CORPORATE YOGI

  • We have developed certain practices based on your lifestyle, so you don’t have to commit for an hour or so, but just 15 mins and slight changes in your lifestyle here and there.
  • To keep your stress away and to keep you healthy we want only 15- 20 mins of your time.

INCREDIBLE YOGI

  • Classes will be designed according to the goal you have in mind.
  • Say you are a dancer, we will analyze the style of dance you do and your physical, emotional and spiritual requirements for that particular style and design a class just for your body type and mind.
  • After all, we want you to excel in your field; that’s our goal.
